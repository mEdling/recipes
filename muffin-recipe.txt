Banana Ginger Muffins
12 muffins or one cake

Muffins:

6dl wheat flour
3 tbsp baking powder
1 tbsp ginger powder
115g brown sugar
75g preserved ginger, chopped
60g butter
2 tbsp honey
125ml milk
2 eggs
240g mashed bananas

Topping:

125g cream cheese, softened
2 tbsp confectioner sugar
2 tsp lemon zest
